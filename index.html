The Pilot

What originally started out as a personal project, born from alight-hearted suggestion from a mate, has turned into something so much more.

Drone photography for me is more than just a hobby. Giving us the ability to see from a different perspective, it is a platform to seeing our world anew. I use it as a way to escape the world of critical thinking and explore my creative avenues. Drone photography is art and technology merging to transform the simplest object into something that fills us with bewonderment
